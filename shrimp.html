Grilled Skewered Honey Shrimp

Description

Show these yummy crustaceans who's boss and enjoy a plate of savory honey grilled shrimp! Onions, peppers, and mushrooms are perfect when alternated with shrimp on the skewers. Can be served with rice or salad or a 32 ounce T-bone steak. Do whatever you want, I don't care!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 teaspoon of Garlic Powder
  • 1/4 tablespoon ground Black Pepper
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire Sauce
  • 1 pound Large Shrimp, peeled and deveined with tails attached
  • 1/4 cup of Honey
  • 1/4 cup of Butter, melted
  • Skewers

Steps

  1. With your father, embark on an epic adventure aboard a shrimping boat and don't come home until you've held captive at least one pound of shrimp. Or go to your local Giant Eagle and head over to the Seafood Section.
  2. In a large bowl, mix together garlic powder, black pepper, and worcestershire; add shrimp and toss to coat. Cover and marinate in the refrigerator for 1 hour.
  3. Preheat the grill to high heat. Thread shrimp onto skewers, piercing once near the tail and once near the head. Discard marinade.
  4. In a small bowl, stir together honey, and melted butter. Set aside for basting.
  5. Lightly oil the grill grate. Grill shrimp for 2 to 3 minutes per side, or until opaque. Baste occasionally with the honey-butter sauce while grilling.
